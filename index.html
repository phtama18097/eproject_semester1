Canon

Founded in 1933 in Tokyo - Japan, Canon has made continuous developments to bring users high quality camera and lens products. Currently Canon is one of the most powerful companies in terms of camera brand and camera products. Camera lenses produced by Canon are always highly appreciated for the quality as well as durability because of the professionalism and famous certainty of Japanese people.

Sony

Sony is a Japanese multinational corporation. Originally called Tokyo Tsushin Kogyo K.K was established in May 1946 in Tokyo - Japan. In January 1958 the company changed its name to Sony. "Sony" is a combination of the Latin "sonus" sound, and the English word "sonny" is a smart boy who is smart in a friendly name. The founders hope "Sony" expresses the spirit of enthusiasm and creativity of young people.

Leica

Leica is an acronym for LEITZ-Camera - a brand of high quality camera that is considered a legend in digital camera technology. Inheriting the scientific techniques and photography passion of engineers working for optical company Ernst Leitz, the optical performance of Leica lenses is very good. Materials that make up Leica lenses are pure glass and undergo rigorous processing. Frames shot with Leica lenses are often very prominent.

Pentax

Pentax was founded in 1919 with the name Asahi Kogaku Goshi Kaisha. In 1938 the company changed its name to Asahi Optical Co., Ltd, at which point the company was producing cameras and lenses for film projectors. In 1952, Asahi Optical produced the first SLR camera in Japan using 35mm film. In 2002 the company changed its name to Pentax and became one of the world's largest photography equipment manufacturing companies.
